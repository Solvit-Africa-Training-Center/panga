For Tenants

Everything you need to know about finding and renting a home.

How do I search for a rental property?

keyboard_arrow_down

You can use the search bar on the homepage to filter properties by location, price, and amenities. We currently list properties across major cities in the Great Lakes region. Use the map view to see listings in specific neighborhoods.

How do I pay my rent securely?

keyboard_arrow_down

GUKODESHA integrates with major mobile money providers in the Great Lakes region (like M-Pesa and Airtel Money) to ensure secure and instant transactions directly to your landlord.

M-Pesa Airtel Money MTN MoMo

What fees are involved in booking?

keyboard_arrow_down

Tenants pay a small service fee upon confirming a rental agreement. This fee covers the cost of identity verification and secure payment processing. The exact amount will be displayed before you confirm any payment.

How do I report a maintenance issue?

keyboard_arrow_down

Log in to your tenant dashboard, select "My Rentals," and click on "Report Issue." You can upload photos and describe the problem. Your landlord will be notified immediately via SMS and email.

Can I cancel a viewing request?

keyboard_arrow_down

Yes, you can cancel up to 1 hour before the scheduled time without any penalty. Go to your "Scheduled Viewings" tab and select cancel.
